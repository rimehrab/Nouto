<p><b>Nouto</b> is a simple and elegant notes app built in Kotlin with Material 3 Expressive Design. It focuses heavily on user experience — fluid transitions, intuitive gestures, and a clean interface that stays out of your way.</p><p>Key features:</p><ul><li><b>Material 3 Expressive design:</b> Sleek and modern UI with dynamic color support and smooth transitions</li><li><b>Dynamic color:</b> Picks up your wallpaper's color scheme on Android 12+ via Material You</li><li><b>Predictive back gestures:</b> Full support for Android 14+ predictive back navigation</li><li><b>Offline-first:</b> Everything is stored locally — no account required, no network calls</li><li><b>Trash:</b> Recover accidentally deleted notes before they're permanently removed</li></ul>
